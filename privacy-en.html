Privacy Policy – EducMathic

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy explains how the application EducMathic handles user data. EducMathic is an educational application designed for children, families, and schools. The app is published by Bail’s Origin, an independent developer.

1. Data Collected

1.1. Data Provided by the User

EducMathic does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

1.2. Automatically Collected Data

Depending on the device configuration and third‑party services, the following data may be collected automatically:

  • Technical device information (model, Android version, language, approximate country)
  • Google Advertising ID (if ads are enabled)
  • Anonymous usage data (screens viewed, interactions, time spent in the app)

2. Use of Data

Any data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving user experience (performance, stability, usability)
  • Displaying advertisements suitable for young audiences (via AdMob)
  • Managing in‑app purchases (Premium Pack)
  • Synchronizing progress if the user chooses to use Google Play Games

3. Local Storage on the Device

EducMathic stores certain information locally on the user’s device:

  • Exercise progress
  • App settings
  • Premium Pack status (if purchased)

None of this data is sent to Bail’s Origin.

4. Third‑Party Services

4.1. Google AdMob

EducMathic uses Google AdMob to display ads suitable for children. Google may collect certain data according to its own privacy policy.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2. Google Play Games Services

If the user chooses to sign in to Google Play Games, some Google account data may be used to:

  • Authenticate the user
  • Save and restore progress
  • Display achievements or leaderboards (if enabled)

This feature is entirely optional.

5. In‑App Purchases

EducMathic offers an in‑app purchase to remove ads and unlock all features. Payments are handled exclusively by Google Play. The app does not process any banking information.

6. Children’s Privacy

EducMathic is designed for young audiences. We comply with the Google Play Families program requirements and applicable child data protection laws.

Ads displayed in the app are configured to be child‑appropriate within the limits of Google AdMob’s available settings.

7. Data Sharing

Data is never sold. It may be shared only with technical service providers such as Google for:

  • Displaying ads
  • Ensuring proper app functionality
  • Managing in‑app purchases

8. User Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, users have rights of access, rectification, deletion, objection, and portability. However, EducMathic does not collect any personal data that directly identifies users. Therefore, these rights do not apply in the context of using the app.

9. Security

Reasonable measures are implemented to protect data. However, no system is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. The date of the latest update is shown at the top of the page.
